What this map is for

This interactive map is made for quick orientation in World of Sea Battle, so you don't have to keep everything in your head or search things in Google. It shows all the main points: cities, ports, islands, forts, lighthouses, PvP zone and resource locations.

Open the map -> enable the filters you need -> instantly see where you should sail.

How to use it

Everything is as simple as possible:

  • The icons on the side are filters by object type. You can show only ports, only resources, or any combination you need.
  • The "Hide all" and "Show all" buttons let you quickly reset or restore everything.
  • Map markers are clickable: you can see the type of location and navigate more easily.
  • A color palette is available to highlight markers for convenience. Highlights are preserved when using filters and after page reloads, but only in your browser.
  • No complex menus or unnecessary actions.

What is marked on the map

The map includes:

  • Cities, ports and bays - the main interaction points. In ports you handle resource trading, crafting, ship changes and other core activities.
  • Personal islands and lighthouses - places where you can quickly travel to a city or build your own personal island.
  • Forts - fort locations, so you can see where to farm them or where it's better to sail around.
  • Resources: coal, iron, wood, resin, copper, water, rum and others - making it easy to plan resource gathering.

Why this saves time

Most unnecessary sailing in the game happens because it's unclear where the required location actually is. With this map, it's easier to:

  • plan your route in advance,
  • find resources faster,
  • and spend less time on "maybe it's somewhere around here".

If something is missing or looks imperfect - that's normal. The map is being updated and refined over time; it's not a static reference. For bugs and suggestions, you can contact us at hi@wosbmap.com.
